SOMETHING YOU DON'T WANT TO MISS IN LIFE

Live your life with no regrets

What do you mean?

Imagine today is the last day of your life, what would you love to do before it ends? Is there some where you would love to visit? Certain food you would like to try? Or someone you really want to see?

Lots of people are working very hard seven days a week, they try to make as much money as they can to buy a house in bay area. Unfortunately this is very unhealthy for their life. They have higher chances to make their life shorter by over working, then whatever rich life becomes zero.

What should you do?

I am not asking you to stop working and go ahead live a life. What I am trying to say here is, you should give yourself a break once in a while. To feel what are the most valuable things in your life.

So what come through your mind right now? Still trying to think? Actually, would you like to start thinking about these?

Run a marathon
Go horseback riding
Do an extreme sport
Visit your grandparents
Connect with past teachers
Give a heartfelt surprise to someone
Be a mentor to someone
Sing to an audience

Believe or not, once you start thinking, you would find out you have already missed a lot of things in the past, and you will feel there are so much stuffs you can not wait to try in life!
